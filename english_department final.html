Explore Our Curriculum ↓

Where words become worlds.

At Terrigal High School, English is more than a subject — it's the foundation of every form of communication, critical thinking, and creative expression your child will need throughout their life.

Our curriculum takes students on a journey from discovering their personal voice in Year 7, all the way to analysing complex literature and crafting sophisticated arguments in Year 12.

📖

Reading

Deep engagement with novels, poetry, drama and film from Australian and world literature.

✍️

Writing

Creative, analytical, persuasive and discursive writing developed across all year levels.

🎤

Speaking

Speeches, debates and presentations build confidence and public communication skills.

🔍

Thinking

Critical analysis, textual concepts and argument skills that serve students in every subject.

What You'll Develop

Skills for Life

✍️

Creative Writing

Develop your own voice through imaginative, narrative and creative writing tasks across all year levels.

🔍

Textual Analysis

Learn to read deeply — analysing language, structure, imagery and context in any text type.

🎤

Public Speaking

Build confidence and clarity through speeches, debates and oral presentations from Year 7 onwards.

🧠

Critical Thinking

Develop sophisticated arguments, evaluate evidence and form well-reasoned perspectives on complex issues.

📜

Essay Writing

Master the craft of analytical and argumentative writing — a skill valued in every profession.

🌏

Cultural Literacy

Explore Australian, Indigenous, and world literature to develop empathy, perspective and cultural understanding.

🎬

Media Literacy

Analyse film, digital media, advertising and visual texts — essential skills for the 21st century.

🤝

Empathy & Perspective

Literature opens windows into other lives, cultures and experiences — building genuine human connection.
